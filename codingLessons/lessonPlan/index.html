About Me

My name is Orla. I am a college student currently pursuing a Gold Gaisce Award. As part of the award, I am undertaking 52 weeks of community involvement. What you see here is my form of community involvement. This website is a handy way to learn to code. Here we'll cover the basic stuff to build smaller projects before moving into more specialised and advanced topics. The course is designed to be easily digestible and each lesson can be covered on a weekly basis. Your time is your own; if you're comfortable to move forward then progress to the next lesson. If you find a topic difficult, or you'd like to know more about it then feel free to keep moving.

I have a number of hobbies in addition to music. I have read 15 books per year the previous two years since college first closed in March 2020. Among my favourite books are John Steinbeck's East of Eden and Fyodor Dostoevsky's Crime and Punishment. My current book count for 2022 is 5 books. In addition to English, I can also speak some Irish and German. I enjoy gaming and hold ten platinum trophies from various PlayStation titles.
