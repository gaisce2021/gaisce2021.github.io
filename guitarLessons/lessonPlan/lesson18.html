Christmas Music - An Overview

Depending on your religion and the country you live in, different times of the year will be highlighted on the calendar. If you don't celebrate Christmas and feel uncomfortable playing music celebrating another religion's holiday, feel free to skip forward to lesson 19 .
Christmas songs are commonly played on the radio, much to the chagrin of retail workers, once Halloween passes. These songs are very familiar to people living in western countries like myself in Ireland. Once you know how the song sounds, you should find it much easier to begin playing yourself.
